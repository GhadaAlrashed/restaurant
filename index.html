About

Ming’s Diner, the Northern Italian restaurant renowned for excellence, has 35 locations worldwide. The largest restaurant opened in November 2016 in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia. The sophisticated Ming’s Diner Restaurant features Italian family recipes passed down from generations and adapted by master chef Mr. Italiano himself and his team of gourmet chefs. The wonderful dishes include creative types of pasta, pizza, . All are brought to life with locally- grown produce and fresh ingredients. In addition to its fine dining restaurant, the Riyadh location includes the more casual dining environment.
